About Our Project

The goal of the flight price prediction project is to develop a model that can accurately predict the prices of airline tickets. This project utilizes data analysis, machine learning, and statistical modeling techniques to analyze historical flight data and make predictions on future flight prices.


The flight price prediction project is aimed at helping travelers plan their trips more effectively by providing insights into the potential price trends of flight tickets. This model predicts the price of the flight based on some parameters like total stops, journey Day, journey month, Air India, Indigo, source, destination, etc.
